About the Course

This course, Graphic Design, is crafted to help you master the essential skills needed to design compelling visuals for both print and digital media. You'll learn the principles of design, including color theory, typography, and layout, as well as software tools like Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign. Through hands-on projects, you'll develop the ability to create impactful designs that effectively communicate messages and captivate audiences.

 

By the end of this course, you will be proficient in creating logos, branding materials, web designs, and marketing graphics. Whether you're a beginner or a professional looking to upgrade your design skills, this course offers a well-rounded approach to mastering graphic design.

What You'll Learn

  • Fundamentals of design theory, including color, typography, and composition
  • Hands-on experience with industry-standard tools like Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign
  • Understanding the principles of branding and visual identity
  • Designing for both print and digital platforms
  • Creating logos, infographics, and marketing materials
  • Capstone project to design a full brand identity and marketing assets
